Supporting Students Through Peer Leadership

Adjusting to a new academic system and cultural environment can be challenging. The Peer Support Network connects incoming international students with experienced senior students at KCGI to provide guidance, encouragement, and practical support.

  • Senior Student Mentors

  • Academic and Daily Life Support
    New students can ask questions about classes, assignments, transportation, housing, and cultural adjustment in a friendly, non-judgmental setting.
  • Leadership Development
    Mentors strengthen leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills while actively contributing to student well-being and campus inclusion.
  • Building Community
    The program encourages cooperation, empathy, and cross-cultural understanding among students from diverse backgrounds.
