Supporting Each Other

Adjusting to a new country can be stressful. The peer support network connects new international students with senior students who can offer guidance and emotional support.

How Peer Support Works

  • Senior students volunteer as mentors
  • New students can ask questions freely
  • Support includes academics, daily life, and cultural adjustment

Leadership and Community

This initiative encourages leadership, empathy, and cooperation within the KCGI international student community.
