Daily life in Japan can feel unfamiliar at first. This section explains essential services and systems to help students settle in smoothly.

Japan has an efficient public transportation system. Students mainly use trains, buses, and IC cards such as ICOCA. These cards can also be used in shops and convenience stores.

Finding housing in Japan may require understanding rental contracts, guarantor systems, and utility payments. Share houses and public housing are popular options for international students because they are more flexible and affordable.
