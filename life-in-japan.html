Daily Life Essentials

Housing

Finding accommodation in Japan can be challenging due to language and contract requirements. Students should carefully check rent, utilities, and house rules before signing any agreement.

Transportation

Public transportation is reliable and widely used in Japan. Students are encouraged to use IC cards such as Suica or Pasmo for trains and buses.

Shopping and Daily Needs

Convenience stores, supermarkets, and 100-yen shops are common and provide affordable daily necessities. Learning basic Japanese phrases can be very helpful.
