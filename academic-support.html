Understanding Academic Culture

Academic communication in Japan may feel different for international students. Respect, punctuality, and clear communication are highly valued.

Class Participation

Students are expected to attend classes regularly, participate in discussions, and complete assignments on time.

Group Work

Group projects are common. Clear communication, responsibility, and mutual respect are essential for successful collaboration.

Communication with Faculty

Emails should be polite and clear. If students are unsure, they are encouraged to ask questions rather than stay silent.
