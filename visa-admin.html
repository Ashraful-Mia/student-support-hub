Important Administrative Information

Residence Card

International students must always carry their residence card. Any change in address should be reported to the local city office.

Visa Renewal

Students should start the visa renewal process well before the expiration date. Required documents may include enrollment certificates and academic records.

City Office Procedures

Many procedures such as health insurance registration and address changes are handled at the city office. Staff may not always speak English, so preparation is important.
