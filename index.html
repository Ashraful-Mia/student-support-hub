Welcome

Moving to a new country for education can be exciting, but also confusing. International students often face challenges related to language, academic systems, housing, transportation, and cultural adjustment.

Why This Project Matters

This Student Support Hub was created to provide clear, practical, and student-friendly guidance for international students at KCGI. The goal is to reduce stress, improve confidence, and build a more inclusive campus community.
