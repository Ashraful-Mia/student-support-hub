Welcome to the Student Support Hub

Starting life in a new country can be exciting but also overwhelming. Many international students at KCGI face challenges related to language, culture, academics, and daily life in Japan.

The Student Support Hub is a digital guide created to help new international students adapt smoothly to life and study at KCGI. This platform provides practical information, academic guidance, and peer support to make the transition easier and more confident.

Who is this for?

  • New international students at KCGI
  • Students struggling with cultural or language barriers
  • Anyone looking for clear guidance about living and studying in Japan
